2) The 1st stroke of the writing system, beginning of the the strokes, the separation of the visible line from blank. It is related to: "God saw that the light was good, and he separated the light from the darkness." (Gen1:4 NIV)

"and let them be lights in the expanse of the sky to give light on the earth." And it was so." (Gen1:15 NIV)

It can link to "And God said, "Let the water teem with living creatures, and let birds fly above the earth across the expanse of the sky."" (Gen1:20 NIV). "So God created the great creatures of the sea and every living and moving thing with which the water teems, according to their kinds, and every winged bird according to its kind. And God saw that it was good." (Gen1:21 NIV)
