Interpretation

1

Optical Illusions:

1) The horizontal line in the middle represents the boundary between upper heaven and lower earth.

Related Verses:
"In the beginning God created the heavens and the earth." (Gen1:1 NIV)

2) The 1st stroke of the writing system, beginning of the the strokes, the separation of the visible line from blank. 

Related Verses:
"God saw that the light was good, and he separated the light from the darkness." (Gen1:4 NIV) 

2

Optical Illusion:

The fist line expand into two. Between the two horizontal lines, are expense or sky.

Related Verses:
"And God said, "Let there be an expanse between the waters to separate water from water."" (Gen1:6 NIV) "So God made the expanse and separated the water under the expanse from the water above it. And it was so." (Gen1:7 NIV)

Optical Illusion

The middle line represents the border between up ground and lower water.

Related Verses:

"And God said, "Let the water under the sky be gathered to one place, and let dry ground appear." And it was so." (Gen1:9 NIV)
